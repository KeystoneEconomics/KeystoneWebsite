About Us

Keystone Economics is a team of economists, analysts, and sector specialists who support organisations in making informed decisions. Our work draws on experience across construction, forecasting, data analysis, and professional consultancy.

We focus on producing analysis that is clear, timely, and relevant. While our work is underpinned by detailed data and forecasting models, our priority is to present findings in a way that decision‑makers can use with confidence.

For over a decade, we have worked with clients through economic cycles, policy change, and periods of uncertainty. This has given us a practical understanding of how economic trends affect real decisions and outcomes.

We are independent and evidence‑led, with a strong commitment to quality and integrity. Our work is robust, transparent, and designed to stand up to scrutiny.
