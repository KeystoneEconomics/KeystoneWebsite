Our Role in EUROCONSTRUCT:

EUROCONSTRUCT brings together 19 national research and consulting organisations, each with an in-depth understanding of its domestic construction market. This structure ensures that forecasts are:

  • Locally informed – based on national expertise and market intelligence
  • Consistent and comparable – using harmonised methodologies across countries
  • Proven and credible – built on nearly five decades of forecasting experience

As the UK partner, Keystone Economics contributes authoritative UK construction market analysis to this respected European network.

Our areas of expertise:

Residential

New and renovation market trends, building permits, starts, completions, housing stock information and much more

Non-Residential

Market trends, focusing on new build public and private sub-sectors such as health, industry, office and commerce.

Civil Engineering

Trends in civil engineering covering traffic, infrastructure, telecom, energy and water works.

Bespoke Research

Tailored economic and construction research, delivering clear, evidence‑based insight for confident decisions.
