About Us

At Keystone Economics, we are a team of highly skilled individuals, with backgrounds spanning construction forecasting, economics, data analytics, and professional consultancy. This breadth of experience allows us to view problems from multiple perspectives, combining economic theory, sector knowledge, and quantitative analysis to deliver well‑rounded, evidence‑based solutions.

Keystone Economics is built on decades of combined experience working across complex markets and challenging environments. Our people have advised clients through economic cycles, shifts in policy, and periods of uncertainty, developing a deep understanding of how economic forces translate into real‑world outcomes.

This experience ensures our work is not only technically robust, but also grounded in the practical realities faced by decision‑makers. We understand the importance of timely, clear, and relevant analysis, and we focus on delivering insight that can be confidently applied in practice. Our team is experienced in handling complex data, developing robust forecasting models, and interpreting economic trends with care and precision.

Our people share a commitment to quality, independence, and integrity. We take pride in producing analysis that is objective, robust, and defensible, always maintaining the highest professional standards.
